Clean Map Project

Mapping garbage locations for a cleaner environment

Our Purpose

Our project aims to create a collaborative platform where users can pinpoint locations that need cleaning due to garbage accumulation. By mapping these areas, we strive to facilitate community-driven clean-up initiatives.

How It Works

Users can mark locations on a map where they observe garbage accumulation. These marked locations can then be accessed by volunteers and organizations looking to organize clean-up drives or contribute to a cleaner environment.

Benefits

  • Empowering communities to take action against environmental pollution
  • Facilitating coordination among volunteers for targeted clean-up efforts
  • Promoting awareness about waste management and environmental stewardship
  • Creating a cleaner and healthier environment for all
